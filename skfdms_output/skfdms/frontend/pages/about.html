About SK-FDMS

The Sangguniang Kabataan Full Disclosure Management System of Barangay Bagtic — promoting transparency, accountability, and youth empowerment in local governance.

What is SK-FDMS?

The SK-FDMS is a web-based platform designed for the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) of Barangay Bagtic, Balilihan, Bohol. It allows SK officials to upload, organize, and publish their official documents online, making them freely accessible to the youth and entire community — anytime, anywhere.

The system was developed in response to the Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) Full Disclosure Policy, which requires all SK units to proactively disclose their financial statements, resolutions, and project reports to the public.

Why It Matters

🌐 Online Access

No need to visit the barangay hall. All SK documents are available online 24/7.

🔒 Data Integrity

Documents are securely stored and cannot be altered after submission.

✅ DILG Compliant

Covers all DILG-required disclosure categories for SK councils.

System Development Timeline

Phase 1 — Requirements Gathering

Interviews with SK Bagtic officials, DILG policy review, and system requirements analysis.

Phase 2 — System Design

Database schema, UI/UX wireframes, 3-tier architecture design.

Phase 3 — Development

Backend API (Node.js/Express), frontend pages, security implementation.

Phase 4 — Testing & Evaluation

Functional, usability, and security testing with Barangay Bagtic SK officials.

Phase 5 — Deployment

Live deployment, SK official training, and system handover.
